Exam Results

SA-I & SA-II result sheets with automatic mark conversion to 50-mark total.

  • FA1 Test: /20 → converted to /10
  • FA2 Test: /20 → converted to /10
  • SA1 Exam: /40 → converted to /20
  • Oral Marks: /10 (direct)
  • Total automatically = /50
  • Grade & Pass/Fail auto-calculated

Data is stored locally in your browser (localStorage). Nothing is sent to any server.
